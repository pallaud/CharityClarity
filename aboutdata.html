About the Data

The data used in the visualizations for Charity Clarity were taken from the financial records published by each organization (American Cancer Society, Autism Speaks, Cancer Fund of America, the Disabled Veterans National Foundation, Feeding America, and Habitat for Humanity) in their annual reports from 2012. U.S. state laws requires any nonprofit of a certain size (threshold varies by state) to submit audited financial statements containing the total revenue or the total contributions received by the charitable nonprofit during the fiscal year. Any submitted financial records are public knowledge, thus making them accessible to anyone. The charities highlighted in Charity Clarity all had the published financial records available on their websites.
